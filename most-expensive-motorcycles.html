15. NCR Leggera 1200 Titanium Special

Price- $1,48,000

NCR Leggera 1200 Titanium Special is one of the most expensive motorcycles in the world.

Based on the Ducati Hypermotard, the superbike is a modified version of the NCR Corse.

This beast weighs 328 lbs. and its 1,200 cc pumps out 132 bhp.

Besides, its other notable features include a titanium frame weighing 10.6 lbs., a full carbon body, carbon-ceramic brakes, zerg silencers, etc.

Further, this superbike is worth $1,48,000.

14. Icon Sheene

Price- $172,000

Only 52 units of these chic retro-looking superbikes were produced.

This crotch rocket beauty boasts Suzuki’s custom-built 1.4 liters turbocharged engine, which gives it a top speed of 200 mph.

Besides, its rear-wheel pumps out 250 bhp and produces 133 lbs-ft of torque.

Its other notable features include stainless steel exhaust, whole carbon fiber built body, and wheels, brand new slip-lock clutch system, Alcantara racing seats, 6-speed quick shift gearbox, etc.

13. MTT Turbine Streetfighter

Price- $175,000

Holding the 13th spot is MTT’s most powerful turbine-powered superbike.

MTT first revealed the Streetfighter in the 2006 Las Vegas SEMA show.

This exclusive beauty boasts a turbine engine that produces 420 hp and 500 lb-ft of torque.

Further, it consists of a large swing arm, solid billets, Pirelli Diablo 240 rear-drive, enhanced aerodynamics, and increased fuel capacity.

The Streetfighter costs a staggering $175,000.

12. Honda RC 213 V-S

Price- $185,000

Honda released its first street-legal Moto GP replica, RC 213 V-S, IN 2015.

Only limited to 250 units worldwide, this stunning beauty boasts a quick shifter modified ECU, a front ram duct, titanium faster, 6-speed sequential manual transmission, stainless steel brakes, etc.

Besides, its 90-degree V4 engine gives it a top speed of more than 299 km/h and pumps out 157 bhp.

Further, this 160 lbs. lightweight superbike is worth $185,000.

11. Suzuki AEM Carbon Fiber Hayabusa

Price- $200,000

Coming to this spot is one of the fastest motorcycle productions in the world.

In 2008, Suzuki revealed AEM Hayabusa at Annual SEMA that took place in Las Vegas.

This carbon fiber-built stunning beauty features tubeless tires, a fine exhaust system, incredible pistons, alloy wheels, and adjustable cam sockets.

Besides, its 1,340 cc liquid-cooled engines give it a top speed of 188 miles per hour.

Further, the Suzuki AEM Carbon Fiber Hayabusa is worth $200,000.

10. Ducati Testa Stretta NCR Macchia Nera

Price- $225,000

Holding the 10th spot is one of the lightest and most sophisticated NCR bikes in the world.

The Italian designer Aldo Drudi made this rare motorcycle with a sleek style in 2003.

Ducati Testa Stretta is made up of carbon fibers, enhanced aerodynamics, and retuned engine head. Besides, this aggressive-looking beast weighs just 297 lbs.

Moreover, its four-stroke V-twin engine produces 180 bhp.

Further, the Ducati Testa Stretta is worth $225,000.

9. Ducati Desmosedici D16RR NCR M16

Price- $235,000

With a price tag of $235,000, the D16RR is the ninth most expensive motorcycle in the world.

The replica of the Desmosedici Moto GP racing bike, D16RR, uses aluminum and titanium materials and thus weighs just 377 lbs.

The Italian brand Ducati produced just 1,500 units of this superbike from 2007 to 2008.

This 989 cc bike can reach a top speed of 188 mph and produce 200 bhp.

It features powerful Ohlins suspension, a fully adjustable rear wheel, Brembo brakes, and dual 4-pistons.

Besides, its whole body is made up of carbon fiber from the swingarm, rear subframes, and fuel tanks to the rims.

8. Ecosse Founder’s Edition TiXX

Price- $300,000

Ecosse Motor Works built this insanely cool bike in 2015.

Ecosse’s exclusive version, TiXX, is one of the fastest bikes in the world, which can reach a top speed of 250 mph.

Besides, it boasts a titanium exhaust system, supercharged intercooler engine, carbon brakes, Ohlins Moto GP racing suspensions, and ceramic hydraulic clutch.

Further, this $300,000 motorcycle can produce 225 bhp and 210 lb-ft of torque.

7. Vincent Black Shadow

Price- $400,000

Vincent Motorcycles built one of the world’s fastest and most expensive bikes in 1948 by producing the classic Vincent Black Shadow.

From 1948 to 1955, the company manufactured three different versions of the Black Shadow.

The rare 988 cc bike features a four-speed Vincent transmission system, aluminum alloy, dry-sump lubrication, and welded box frame.

Besides, the bike weighs 458 lbs. and can reach a top speed of 125 mph while pumping out 55 bhp.

6. Dodge Tomahawk V10 Superbike

Price- $555,000

The Dodge Vehicles made history by producing one of the world’s most bold and marvelous bikes, the Tomahawk.

They produced only nine units of the superbike from 2003 to 2006. And sold them for a whopping $555,000.

This prominent bike features two-speed manual transmissions, front-horizontal double fork suspension, dry-sump lubrication, a rear suspension lock, etc.

Further, its 8.3 L V-10 engine can reach a top speed of 420 mph and produce 500 bhp with 525 lbs-ft of torque.

5. Harley Davidson Cosmic Starship

Price- $1.5 Million

Holding the fifth spot is one of the most futuristic superbikes in the world.

Harley Davidson, one of the most renowned chopper bikes manufacturers in the world, built this chic-looking bike in 2002.

This 1,499 cc beast features Harley’s special V-Rod, double overhead camshafts, a liquid-cooled engine, and six forward gears.

While the most fascinating part of this bike is its cosmic acrylic which Jack Armstrong, Neil Armstrong’s nephew, made by coating at least 37 coats on the top portion of the bike.

Further, this unusual Harley’s cosmic starship is worth a whopping $1.5 million.

4. The Yamaha BMS Chopper

Price- $3 Million

With a price tag of $3 million, Yamaha’s BMS Chopper is the fourth most expensive motorcycle in the world.

This velvety red beauty boasts a 24-karat gold plated body, a 360 mm rear tire, double exhaust system, and rear fender edges accompanied with LED brake lights.

Further, its 1,700 cc V-twin engine produces 200 bhp.

3. Ecosse ES1 Spirit

Price- $3.6 Million

Ecosse’s incredible bike, ES1 Spirit, is the world’s third most expensive motorcycle.

This gorgeous bike comes in tangerine, and silvery shade, weighs just 265 lbs. and can reach a top speed of 250 mph.

Besides, it features a full carbon body, titanium frames, a double aerodynamics system, a single-sided swingarm, and ceramic composite brakes.

Further, this four-stroke engine bike produces more than 200 bhp and is worth $3.6 million.

2. 1949 E90 AJS Porcupine

Price- $7 Million

This ultra-rare beauty won the 1949 Moto GP Championship, the first world racing championship held for the 500 cc motorbikes.

Besides, it was the only twin-cylinder bike to win the world title.

The British company AMC produced AJS Porcupine in 1949. It can reach a top speed of 135 mph and pump out 48 bhp.

Further, it features an E90s engine, air cooler, high fuel pumping capacity, horizontal cylinders, and cool spiked head finning.

As of 2022, the Porcupine is worth $7 million.

1. Neiman Marcus Limited Edition Fighter

Price- $11 Million

At the top comes the out worldly seductive streetfighter motorcycle.

The US-based Neiman Marcus made just 45 units of this silver street-legal fighter bike worth a whopping $11 million.

This ultra-fast beast boasts a V-twin engine with an air cooler that can reach a top speed of 300 km/hr.

It features a seat for a single rider only.

Besides, this rare edition is made up of light carbon frames consisting of both aluminum and titanium and has wet-sump lubrication, six-speed transmissions, and a custom brake system.

The motorcycle is valued at a whopping $11 million.
