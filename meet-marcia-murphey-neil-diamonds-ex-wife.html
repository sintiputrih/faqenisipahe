Marcia Murphey Met Diamond Murphey While Working at a TV Station

It was while Marcia Murphey was working as part of the crew of Clay Cole Show that she saw multiple award-winning musician Diamond Murphey for the first time. When Fred Weintraubb’s “Live From the Bitter End” series hired her services, the duo started seeing more of each other

The American musician described his ex-wife as a lady who obviously understood great pain. The duo didn’t get to talk at the point of their first meeting, however, Marcia, who was already married and divorced, wasn’t deterred to find out that Diamond was a married man and still went ahead to date him.

Diamond’s first wife, Jaye Posner, was his college sweetheart, and they had already been married for four years, from 1963 to 1967, with two children – Elyn and Marjorie. Finding out all these details didn’t douse Marcia’s interest in the musician one bit, and she patiently waited for Diamond to end things with Jaye before they became officially engaged. The American singer once revealed how he went out for a pack of cigarettes one day and decided never to go back home again, and that marked the end of his first marriage.

She was Neil Diamond’s Wife for 25 Years

After Diamond walked out on his marriage with Jaye in 1967, the couple finalized their divorce a couple of years later in 1969. That same year, Marcia Murphey and her boyfriend, Diamond, exchanged their forever commitment on December 5th, 1969.

Marcia and Diamond decided to sell the house they were living in after their nuptials. They moved to another plush abode on Malibu beach located up the roadside from the Peters/Streisand summer place.

While their union lasted, love blossomed between the couple. When Diamond released the hit song “Sweet Caroline,” fans were quick to assume that it was dedicated to President John F. Kennedy’s daughter, Caroline Kennedy. However, Diamond later came up with a clarification saying that he wrote the song for Marcia, but her name didn’t rhyme with the beats, thus, he decided to go with Carolina.

Talking about the years he spent with Marcia in a 1992 interview session, the American music star admitted that he was not an easy person to cope with, but Marcia was able to handle all his shortcomings for 25 years.

After 25 long years of marriage, the couple decided to call it quits on their relationship and filed for divorce in 1994.

Marcia Murphey and Diamond Murphey Share Two Children

Marcia Murphey and her ex-husband Diamond received their first child after being married for two years. The couple’s first son, Jesse Diamond, was born in 1970, however, his exact date of birth is not known.

Following the birth of their first son, Diamond vowed to Marcia that he would be the best husband and father and spend quality time with her and their kids, something he never did during his first marriage to Jaye.

Standing by his vows, Diamond cut ties with his professional life and halted all public appearances. For the ensuing four years, the music hitmaker didn’t stage any live performances, rather, he took to fishing and other leisure activities just to be close to his family.

Jesse Diamond was going to seven years when he became a big brother to the couple’s second son, Micah Diamond, who announced his entrance sometime in 1978.

By this time, Diamond was already back on the stage, and since 1976 was playing to sell-out crowds, yet, he still gave priority to his family with Marcia Murphey. The popular musician made sure to return home after each tour and was full of praises for the mother of children, describing Marcia as The “rock on which his life is anchored”. Diamond also said Marcia was a whole lot saner than him and helps in keeping his sanity intact.

Marcia and Diamond don’t just share children, the former couple equally share a grandchild. Their first son Jesse got married to actress Sheryl Lee, and they have a son called Elijah Diamond.

Diamond and Marcia’s children are obviously fond of their parents and never forget to wish them well on their anniversaries.

The Marcia Murphey and Diamond Murphey’s Divorce Settlement Sparked a Lot of Talk from Fans

With Marcia Murphey and Diamond Murphey’s divorce done and dusted, speculations started making the rounds that Marcie received a whopping $150 million from her ex-husband as a divorce settlement. While all these heated debates were going on, Diamond was still devastated by his divorce and didn’t deem it necessary to come up with a confirmation or rebuttal of the said amount. However, he was able to pick up the pieces of his life together and decided to break his silence about the rumors.

The American music superstar chose to talk to Dailymail, saying that the amount people were claiming as Marcia Murphey’s alimony didn’t represent the real settlement he made to her. In conclusion, Diamond said musicians don’t earn such huge amounts, which he said can only be made by thieves and businessmen.

Talking about the undisclosed amount he did pay as alimony, the celebrated musician said he had no qualms about paying out such money because he felt so guilty for how he treated Marcia.

In recent times, Marcia surfaced on her son Jesse’s Instagram handle, where he wished her a “Happy Mother’s Day celebration” in 2022

Did Marcia Murphey Remarry?

Since she got divorced from Diamond in 1995, Marcia Murphey has not deemed it necessary to go into another relationship, and to date, she never married again.

However, the same cannot be said about her celebrity ex-husband, who got married seven years later. Diamond’s second wife has been identified as Katie McNeil

Their nuptials were hosted in Los Angeles, and Diamond took to Twitter to announce it to fans the next day, describing the moment as magical. The singer has since released an album, Melody Road, which he dedicated to Katie.
