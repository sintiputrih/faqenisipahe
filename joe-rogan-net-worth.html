Bell Canyon House

This custom-made house in Bell Canyon, California was purchased by Joe Rogan 18 years ago for $2.33 million.

The French Country-style house features five bedrooms and five bathrooms. Only the living space has spanned over 7,500 square feet of area.

Initially, this house was a habitat for 22 egg-laying chickens, and after purchasing it, the couple renovated the house.

The house sports an open floor plan where you can have a great sitting room, living room, and dining room. Moreover, the house boasts a library, an office, a meditation space, a playroom, a wet bar, and an MMA gym.

Inside the kitchen, you can spot a 9′ antique pewter center island, cute cabinets, marble countertops, and a gorgeous chandelier hanging from the ceiling. Furthermore, there are two Miele refrigerators and three sinks along with several high-end appliances.

If we are going to talk about the backyard, it’s one of the main highlights of the estate. The view of the backyard is stunning, with a sparkling inground pool and a spa looking over beautiful mountains.
